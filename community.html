How the community is run

Moderated, intentional, and never an open mic.

The Rise Ready Community is hosted on Circle.so and moderated by Nicole and a team of three community leads. Membership is curated — every applicant is reviewed, and every space is governed by published guidelines. The result is a room where Black and brown women can speak honestly about work without performing for anyone outside it.

  • →
    Application required Every member is reviewed. The community is not a public Facebook group.
  • →
    Moderated, not policed Three community leads keep conversation generative and safe. Receipts kept. Standards held.
  • →
    Wins are opt-in Members decide when, how, and whether their wins appear publicly. Default is private.
