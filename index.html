What is Rise Ready?

A movement built for the women systems were never designed to hold.

Rise Ready is the only self-advocacy ecosystem built explicitly by and for Black and brown women. Founded by Nicole Paultre Bell, it pairs evidence-based curriculum with a fierce community and the data infrastructure to prove what the rest of the industry refuses to measure.

We exist for the women who are tired of being the only one in the room — and who are ready to change which rooms exist. Every course, every cohort, and every sponsor we partner with has to answer to that mission.
