Frequently Asked

Booking, logistics, and the answers we get every week.

What is Nicole's typical availability?
Nicole accepts approximately 24 keynotes per calendar year. The current booking window is 6–9 months out for Q1–Q2 dates, 4–6 months out for Q3–Q4. Confirmed dates are listed on the booking calendar shared after intake submission.
Does Nicole offer virtual keynotes?
Yes — virtual keynotes are available at a 30% reduced rate from the in-person fee range listed for each talk. Hybrid (in-person + virtual stream) keynotes are charged at the full in-person rate.
What's the booking timeline once we submit the intake form?
You'll receive an automated personalized preview within 60 seconds. A member of Nicole's booking team follows up with a personal note and date confirmation within 24 hours. Contracts are issued within 5 business days of date confirmation.
Can Nicole customize a talk for our specific audience?
Yes. Every signature talk includes a 60-minute prep call with the event lead to tailor opening, examples, and audience callouts. Fully custom talks (a new framework not in the signature menu) carry an additional development fee, scoped on the prep call.
What are Nicole's travel and AV requirements?
Domestic: economy-plus or premium economy, single hotel room (4-star or equivalent), green room with water and a quiet space 60 minutes pre-keynote. International: business class, two nights' accommodation. Full AV rider provided in the speaker kit PDF.
Are there any topics Nicole doesn't speak on?
Nicole speaks at the intersection of self-advocacy, women's leadership, and policy. She does not accept engagements that frame her work as "diversity training" without substantive change components, and does not speak at events that exclude or token-cast Black and brown women audiences.
