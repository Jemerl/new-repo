Signature Talks

Choose the talk that fits your stage.

Each signature talk can be delivered as a 45–60 minute keynote, a 90-minute interactive workshop, or a 30-minute panel anchor. Custom talks available for confirmed engagements.

Keynote · 45–60 min $15K–$30K

The Aftershock Effect

Nicole's signature talk based on the documentary. How appearance-based discrimination operates invisibly in corporate spaces, what it costs organizations, and what Black and brown women can do about it. Includes live data from the Aftershock Index.

Best for: corporate women's conferences, DEI summits, HR leadership gatherings.

Keynote / Workshop $10K–$20K

Find Your Voice in the Room That Matters

Interactive self-advocacy session. Audience members leave with three specific language tools they can use in their next professional encounter — scripts, not platitudes.

Best for: university audiences, ERG events, women's leadership summits.

Policy Keynote · 30–45 min $15K–$25K

The CROWN Act and the Boardroom

Nicole's legislative expertise applied to corporate context. What the CROWN Act means for employers and employees, the business case for appearance-equity policies, and what organizations can do today.

Best for: HR conferences, legal/compliance audiences, DEI leadership teams.

Commencement · 20–30 min $8K–$15K

Run For Something: Women, Power, and Public Life

Nicole's public leadership journey for students and emerging leaders at the inflection point of choosing public vs. private careers. Direct, tactical, unforgettable.

Best for: university commencements, honor societies, leadership institutes.
